### Description (4000 characters max)

```
4 Paws is Australia's first and only cat sitting marketplace built exclusively for cat owners and cat lovers. Whether you're going on holiday, working late, or need someone to check on your fur baby, 4 Paws connects you with trusted, verified cat sitters in your neighbourhood.

WHY CAT OWNERS LOVE 4 PAWS

Unlike generic pet sitting apps, 4 Paws is designed from the ground up for cats. Every sitter on our platform is a verified cat specialist who understands feline behaviour, dietary needs, and the importance of keeping your cat comfortable in their own home.

FIND THE PERFECT SITTER

Browse cat sitters near you using our interactive map and smart filters. Search by location (5km, 10km, 20km, or 50km radius), availability dates, price range, rating, and special skills like administering medication or caring for senior cats. Every sitter profile includes verified reviews, qualifications, and response times.

REAL-TIME GPS GEO-FENCING

Set a safe zone around your home and receive instant alerts if your cat's sitter moves outside the designated area. Our geo-fencing technology gives you peace of mind that your cat is safe and sound, even when you're on the other side of the world.

SECURE IN-APP PAYMENTS

Pay securely through the app with Stripe. No cash, no awkward conversations. See transparent pricing upfront with no hidden fees. Sitters set their own rates, and you'll always know exactly what you're paying before you book.

REAL-TIME CHAT & UPDATES

Stay connected with your sitter through instant messaging. Receive photo updates, ask questions, and get real-time notifications about your cat's care. Our chat includes typing indicators and read receipts so you always know when your sitter has seen your message.

VERIFIED & TRUSTED SITTERS

Every sitter goes through our verification process including identity checks, a cat care questionnaire, and document verification. We take safety seriously — your cat deserves the best.

KEY FEATURES
• Interactive map to find sitters near you
• GPS geo-fencing with real-time alerts
• Smart filters: location, dates, price, rating, skills
• Secure Stripe payments with transparent pricing
• Real-time chat with photo sharing
• Push notifications for bookings and updates
• Detailed sitter profiles with verified reviews
• Cat profiles to share your cat's needs
• Dark mode support
• Emergency contact system

GETTING STARTED IS EASY

1. Sign up as a Cat Owner or Cat Sitter
2. Set your location and browse nearby sitters
3. Book, pay, and chat — all in the app
4. Leave a review after your sitting is complete

Download 4 Paws today and give your cat the care they deserve.
```
