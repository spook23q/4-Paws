## Welcome to 4 Paws

4 Paws is a dedicated cat sitting marketplace built exclusively for cats. Unlike general pet sitting platforms that cater to dogs, birds, and reptiles alongside cats, 4 Paws ensures that every sitter on the platform specialises in feline care. The app connects cat owners with trusted, verified cat sitters in the Sydney metro area, providing a seamless experience from searching for a sitter to completing payment and leaving a review.

The platform operates on a transparent pricing model: cat owners pay a flat **$3 booking fee** per booking on top of the sitter's rate, and sitters receive their earnings minus a 15% platform service fee. There are no hidden charges, no subscription fees, and no registration costs.

### Key Features at a Glance

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Cat-Only Focus** | Every sitter specialises exclusively in cat care |
| **Verified Sitters** | Compliance checks including WWCC, First Aid, and ABN verification |
| **Real-Time Chat** | Instant messaging with typing indicators, read receipts, and online status |
| **Secure Payments** | Stripe-powered payments with full cost breakdown |
| **Geo-Fencing** | Service area restricted to Sydney metro for quality assurance |
| **Availability Calendar** | Sitters manage their availability; owners filter by date |
| **Distance-Based Search** | Find sitters within 5km, 10km, 20km, or 50km of your location |
| **Push Notifications** | Real-time alerts for bookings, messages, and compliance updates |
| **AI Chatbot (PawsBot)** | 24/7 support for cat care questions and app guidance |
| **Dark Mode** | Full light and dark theme support |

## Getting Started

### Downloading the App

4 Paws is available on both iOS and Android. Download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store by searching for "4 Paws" or "4 Paws Cat Sitting."

### Creating Your Account

When you first open the app, you will see the welcome screen featuring the 4 Paws logo, the tagline "Paws & Peace of Mind — Cats Only," and a **Get Started** button.

**Step 1 — Choose Your Role.** Tap "Get Started" to proceed to the role selection screen. You will be presented with two options:

- **I'm a Cat Owner** — Choose this if you are looking for someone to care for your cat while you are away.
- **I'm a Cat Sitter** — Choose this if you want to offer your cat sitting services on the platform.

You can see the transparent pricing information on this screen: owners pay just $3 per booking, and sitters earn money caring for cats.

**Step 2 — Sign Up.** Fill in your details on the registration form:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Full Name | Yes | Your display name on the platform |
| Email | Yes | Used for login and account recovery |
| Phone | No | Optional contact number |
| Password | Yes | Minimum 6 characters |
| Confirm Password | Yes | Must match password |

Tap **Create Account** to complete registration. You will be automatically signed in and taken to the home screen.

**Step 3 — Set Up Your Profile.** After signing up, navigate to the **Profile** tab to complete your profile. Add your profile photo by tapping the avatar circle, fill in your personal details, and set your address. Your address is used for distance-based sitter search and geo-fencing validation.

### Signing In

If you already have an account, tap **Sign In** on the welcome screen. Enter your email and password, and optionally check **Remember Me** to stay signed in across app restarts. If you forget your password, tap **Forgot Password?** to receive a password reset link.

## For Cat Owners

### Home Screen

The owner home screen serves as your central dashboard. It displays:

- **Quick Actions** — Shortcuts to Find a Sitter, My Bookings, Messages, and Payments.
- **Upcoming Bookings** — A preview of your next scheduled booking with sitter details.
- **Pricing & Fees** — A transparent breakdown of the $3 per-booking fee, typical sitter rates, and accepted payment methods.
- **Notification Bell** — Located in the header, showing unread notification count with a red badge.

### Finding a Cat Sitter

Navigate to the **Search** tab to browse available cat sitters. The search screen provides powerful filtering and sorting capabilities.

**Search Filters.** Tap the filter icon to expand the full filter panel:

| Filter | Options | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Distance** | 5km, 10km, 20km, 50km | Radius from your saved address |
| **Availability** | Tomorrow, This Week, This Month, Custom Dates | Only shows sitters available for your dates |
| **Price Range** | Slider ($0–$200) | Filter by daily/nightly rate |
| **Minimum Rating** | 1–5 stars | Only show sitters above this rating |
| **Special Skills** | Medication, Multiple Cats, Kittens, Senior Cats, Special Needs | Filter by sitter capabilities |

**Sorting Options.** Sort results by Distance (nearest first), Rating (highest first), or Price (lowest first).

**Active Filter Badge.** When filters are applied, a badge on the filter icon shows how many filters are active. Tap **Clear All Filters** to reset.

**Map View.** Tap the map icon to switch from list view to an interactive map showing sitter locations as markers. Tap a marker to see the sitter's summary, then tap again to view their full profile.

### Viewing a Sitter Profile

Tap any sitter card to open their detailed profile. The sitter detail screen includes:

- **Profile photo, name, and suburb** with verification badges.
- **Bio and experience** — The sitter's introduction and years of experience.
- **Pricing** — Day rate and overnight rate clearly displayed.
- **Special skills** — Icons showing capabilities like medication administration or kitten care.
- **Availability calendar** — A month view showing which dates the sitter is available (green) or unavailable (grey).
- **Reviews** — Star ratings and written reviews from previous clients.
- **Action buttons** — "Book Now" to start a booking, "Message" to start a chat, and a heart icon to save the sitter to your favourites.

### Booking a Cat Sitter

**Step 1 — Select Dates.** From the sitter detail screen, tap **Book Now**. Choose your start and end dates using the date picker. The booking form shows the sitter's rate and calculates the total automatically.

**Step 2 — Select Your Cats.** Choose which of your registered cats need sitting. If you haven't added any cats yet, you will be prompted to add one first.

**Step 3 — Review Cost Breakdown.** Before confirming, you will see a detailed cost breakdown:

| Item | Example |
|------|---------|
| Sitter Rate | $35/day x 3 days = $105.00 |
| Booking Fee | $3.00 |
| Service Fee (15%) | $15.75 |
| **Total** | **$123.75** |

**Step 4 — Pay Booking Fee.** You must pay the $3 booking fee via Stripe before the booking request is submitted. Enter your card details or use a saved payment method.

**Step 5 — Await Confirmation.** Your booking request is sent to the sitter. You will receive a push notification when the sitter accepts or declines.

### Managing Bookings

Navigate to the **Bookings** tab to view all your bookings organised into tabs:

- **Upcoming** — Confirmed bookings that haven't started yet.
- **Pending** — Booking requests awaiting sitter response.
- **Past** — Completed or cancelled bookings.
- **Payments** — Full payment history with transaction details, amounts, and statuses.

Tap any booking to view its details. From the booking detail screen you can:

- **Cancel** a pending or upcoming booking.
- **Message** the sitter directly.
- **Pay** the sitter's fee (for confirmed bookings).
- **Leave a Review** after the booking is completed.

### Leaving a Review

After a booking is completed, you will be prompted to leave a review. Rate the sitter from 1 to 5 stars and write a text review describing your experience. Reviews are displayed on the sitter's public profile and help other cat owners make informed decisions.

### Managing Your Cats

Navigate to **Profile > My Pets** to manage your cat profiles. Each cat profile includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Name | Your cat's name |
| Age | Age in years |
| Breed | Breed or "Mixed" |
| Temperament | e.g., Friendly, Shy, Playful |
| Medical Notes | Allergies, medications, conditions |
| Feeding Schedule | Meal times and food preferences |
| Photo | Upload a photo of your cat |

Tap **Add Cat** to register a new cat, or tap an existing cat to edit their details. You can select which cats need sitting when creating a booking.

### Favourite Sitters

Save sitters you like by tapping the heart icon on their profile. Access your saved sitters from **Profile > Favourites**. The favourites list shows each sitter's name, suburb, rating, and rate for quick reference.

## For Cat Sitters

### Getting Started as a Sitter

After selecting "I'm a Cat Sitter" during registration, you will be guided through additional onboarding steps.

**Sitter Questionnaire.** Complete the compliance questionnaire covering:

- **Work Status** — Your eligibility to work in Australia.
- **ABN** — Australian Business Number (required for tax purposes).
- **Working With Children Check (WWCC)** — Required for child safety compliance.
- **Experience** — Your cat care experience and qualifications.

**Profile Setup.** Complete your sitter profile with:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Bio | A personal introduction for cat owners |
| Experience | Years of cat care experience |
| Day Rate | Your rate for daytime visits ($/day) |
| Overnight Rate | Your rate for overnight stays ($/night) |
| Service Area | Your suburb and service radius |
| Special Skills | Medication, kittens, senior cats, multiple cats, special needs |
| Profile Photo | A professional photo of yourself |

### Sitter Home Screen

The sitter home screen displays a dashboard tailored to your role:

- **Dashboard Stats** — Total bookings, average rating, and total earnings.
- **Compliance Status** — A banner showing your verification status. A green banner means you are fully compliant. A yellow banner indicates pending documents. A red banner means you have missing or expired documents and cannot accept bookings.
- **Upcoming Jobs** — Your next scheduled bookings.
- **Quick Actions** — Shortcuts to My Availability, My Jobs, Messages, and Compliance Documents.

### Managing Availability

Navigate to **Profile > My Availability** to open the availability calendar. This is a month-view calendar where you can:

- **Tap dates** to toggle them between available (green) and unavailable (default).
- **Select date ranges** by tapping a start date and then an end date.
- **Remove availability** for dates you previously marked as available.

Cat owners can see your availability calendar on your profile and filter search results to only show sitters available on their requested dates.

### Managing Booking Requests

When a cat owner requests a booking with you, you will receive a push notification and an in-app notification. Navigate to **Jobs** (the Bookings tab for sitters) to view:

- **Requests** — New booking requests awaiting your response. Each request shows the owner's name, their cats, requested dates, and the total payment amount.
- **Upcoming** — Bookings you have accepted that are scheduled.
- **Past** — Completed bookings.

For each request, you can:

- **Accept** — Confirm the booking. The owner will be notified and can proceed with payment. Note: you must have valid compliance documents (WWCC at minimum) to accept bookings.
- **Decline** — Reject the booking request. The owner will be notified.
- **Mark Complete** — After the sitting period ends, mark the booking as complete to trigger the review prompt for the owner.

### Compliance Documents

Navigate to **Profile > Compliance Documents** to upload and manage your verification documents. The following document types are supported:

| Document | Required | Description |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Working With Children Check (WWCC) | Recommended | Child safety verification |
| First Aid Certificate | Optional | Current first aid qualification |
| Pet First Aid Certificate | Optional | Pet-specific first aid training |
| Animal Care Certificate | Optional | Formal animal care qualification |
| Public Liability Insurance | Optional | Professional insurance coverage |
| ABN Certificate | Recommended | Australian Business Number proof |

For each document, you upload the file (photo or PDF), enter the reference number, and set the issue and expiry dates. Documents go through a verification process:

- **Pending** — Uploaded and awaiting admin review.
- **Verified** — Approved by the admin team.
- **Rejected** — Not accepted (you will receive a notification with the reason).
- **Expired** — Past the expiry date (you will receive a reminder 30 days before expiry).

### Sitter Earnings

Sitters receive payment for completed bookings minus the 15% platform service fee. Payments are processed through Stripe Connect. From the sitter home screen, you can view your earnings summary and payout history.

## Real-Time Chat

### Starting a Conversation

You can start a conversation with a sitter or owner from several places:

- **Sitter Profile** — Tap the "Message" button on any sitter's detail page.
- **Booking Detail** — Tap "Message" on any booking to chat with the other party.
- **Messages Tab** — View all your existing conversations.

### Chat Features

The messaging system provides a full real-time chat experience:

- **Instant Delivery** — Messages are delivered in real time via WebSocket (Socket.IO). No need to refresh.
- **Typing Indicators** — See when the other person is typing, with debounced updates to avoid flickering.
- **Read Receipts** — Double ticks appear when your message has been read by the recipient.
- **Online Status** — A green dot next to the user's name indicates they are currently online.
- **Connection Status** — The header shows "Reconnecting..." if the connection drops, so you always know the current state.
- **Relative Timestamps** — Messages show "Just now," "5m ago," "2h ago," etc., for easy time reference.
- **Push Notification Fallback** — If the recipient is offline, they receive a push notification for new messages.

### Conversation List

The **Messages** tab shows all your conversations sorted by most recent activity. Each conversation card displays:

- The other person's name and profile photo.
- The last message preview (prefixed with "You:" if you sent it).
- A green online indicator if they are currently active.
- An unread message count badge.

### Notification Sounds

Chat messages, booking updates, and general alerts each have distinct notification sounds. You can customise these in **Profile > Notification Sounds**, where you can:

- Toggle notification sounds on or off.
- Adjust the volume with a slider.
- Preview each sound type (message, booking, general alert).

## Payments

### Payment Methods

All payments on 4 Paws are processed securely through **Stripe**. You can pay using any major credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). Payment details are handled entirely by Stripe and are never stored on 4 Paws servers.

### Cost Structure for Cat Owners

| Fee | Amount | Description |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| **Booking Fee** | $3.00 per booking | Flat fee charged when you submit a booking request |
| **Sitter Rate** | Varies | The sitter's daily or nightly rate multiplied by the number of days |
| **Service Fee** | 15% of sitter rate | Platform service fee |

The full cost breakdown is displayed before you confirm any payment, so there are no surprises.

### Payment Flow

1. **Booking Fee ($3)** — Paid upfront when you submit a booking request. This is charged immediately via Stripe.
2. **Sitter Payment** — After the sitter accepts your booking, you can pay the sitter's fee from the booking detail screen. The payment screen shows the full breakdown (sitter rate, service fee, total).

### Payment History

Navigate to **Bookings > Payments** tab to view your complete transaction history. Each entry shows the booking reference, amount, date, and payment status (Confirmed, Pending, or Failed).

### Sitter Payouts

Sitters receive their earnings through Stripe Connect. The platform deducts a 15% service fee from each booking payment. Sitters can view their balance, payout history, and pending transfers from their dashboard.

## Safety and Trust

### Geo-Fencing

4 Paws currently operates exclusively within the **Sydney metro area**. This geo-fence ensures:

- **Quality Control** — The platform can maintain high service standards within a defined area.
- **Address Validation** — When you set your address, the app verifies it falls within the Sydney metro boundary. Addresses outside this area are not accepted.
- **Sitter Proximity** — Distance-based search ensures you are connected with sitters who are genuinely nearby.

The geo-fence boundary covers approximately 80km from Sydney CBD, encompassing suburbs from the Northern Beaches to Sutherland, and from the Blue Mountains foothills to the Eastern Suburbs.

### Sitter Verification

All cat sitters on 4 Paws go through a compliance verification process. Sitters are encouraged to upload documents including their Working With Children Check, First Aid certificates, ABN, and professional insurance. These documents are reviewed by the 4 Paws admin team and marked as verified, ensuring that cat owners can trust the sitters they book.

Sitters with missing or expired critical documents are blocked from accepting new bookings until their compliance is restored. Verification badges are displayed on sitter profiles so owners can see at a glance which sitters are fully verified.

### Emergency Contact

The app includes an **Emergency Contact** feature accessible from the home screen. In case of an emergency during a sitting, you can quickly access emergency contact information and make a one-tap call.

### Data Privacy

4 Paws takes your privacy seriously. The app includes a comprehensive **Privacy Policy** (accessible from Profile > Privacy Policy) and complies with data protection regulations. You can request account deletion at any time (see Account Management below), which removes all your personal data from the platform.

## Account Management

### Editing Your Profile

Navigate to the **Profile** tab to view and edit your account information. The profile screen is organised into sections:

- **About Me** — Edit your name, email, phone, and role.
- **My Pets** — Manage your cat profiles (owners only).
- **My Address** — Set or update your address for distance-based search.
- **Favourites** — View your saved sitters (owners only).
- **Account** — Light/dark mode toggle, notification sounds, and account actions.
- **App Info** — Links to About Us, FAQ, Safety & Trust, Why Choose Us, Pricing Comparison, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Service.

### Changing Your Address

Navigate to **Profile > My Address** to update your location. Enter your street address, suburb, state, postcode, and country. The app validates that your address falls within the Sydney metro geo-fence. Your address is used to calculate distances to sitters and to auto-fill the suburb filter in search.

### Light and Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark mode from **Profile > Account > Dark Mode**. Your preference is saved and persists across app restarts. The app defaults to light mode. All screens, including chat, bookings, and search, adapt their colours automatically.

### Deleting Your Account

If you wish to delete your account, navigate to **Profile > Account > Delete Account**. You will be asked to:

1. Enter your password for verification.
2. Type "DELETE" to confirm.

Account deletion is permanent and removes all your personal data, bookings, messages, and reviews from the platform. This action cannot be undone.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What makes 4 Paws different from other pet sitting apps?**

4 Paws is built exclusively for cats. Every sitter on the platform specialises in feline care, which means your cat receives attention from someone who understands cat behaviour, dietary needs, and medical requirements. General pet sitting platforms spread their focus across dogs, birds, reptiles, and more, which dilutes the quality of cat-specific care.

**How much does it cost to use 4 Paws?**

Cat owners pay a flat $3 booking fee per booking, plus the sitter's rate and a 15% service fee. There are no registration fees, no subscription charges, and no hidden costs. The full cost breakdown is shown before you confirm any payment.

**How are sitters verified?**

Sitters complete a compliance questionnaire during onboarding and are encouraged to upload verification documents (WWCC, First Aid, ABN, insurance). These documents are reviewed by the 4 Paws admin team. Verified sitters display verification badges on their profiles.

**What area does 4 Paws cover?**

4 Paws currently operates within the Sydney metro area, covering approximately 80km from the CBD. This includes suburbs from the Northern Beaches to Sutherland and from the Blue Mountains foothills to the Eastern Suburbs.

**Can I use 4 Paws on both iOS and Android?**

Yes. 4 Paws is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. The app also supports landscape orientation on tablets and larger devices.

**What happens if a sitter cancels?**

If a sitter declines or cancels a booking, you will receive a push notification and an in-app notification immediately. You can then search for another available sitter.

**How do I contact support?**

You can use the **PawsBot** AI chatbot (accessible via the floating chat button or the PawsBot tab) for instant answers to common questions. For more complex issues, navigate to **Profile > Contact Us** to reach the 4 Paws support team.

**Can I book multiple cats with one sitter?**

Yes. When creating a booking, you can select multiple cats from your registered cat profiles. The sitter will see all selected cats and their care requirements.

**How do sitters get paid?**

Sitters receive payments through Stripe Connect. After a booking is completed and the owner pays, the sitter's earnings (minus the 15% platform fee) are transferred to their Stripe account.

## Troubleshooting

**I can't sign in to my account.**
Ensure you are using the correct email and password. If you have forgotten your password, tap "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in screen. Check that you have a stable internet connection.

**I'm not receiving push notifications.**
Ensure notifications are enabled for the 4 Paws app in your device settings (Settings > Notifications > 4 Paws). Check that notification sounds are enabled in the app (Profile > Notification Sounds). If you are on Android, ensure battery optimisation is not blocking the app.

**The map is not loading.**
The map feature requires location permissions. Go to your device settings and ensure 4 Paws has permission to access your location. On some devices, you may need to select "While Using the App" rather than "Never."

**My address is being rejected.**
4 Paws currently operates within the Sydney metro area only. If your address is outside this boundary, it will not be accepted. Ensure you are entering a valid Sydney metro address.

**Payment failed.**
Check that your card details are correct and that you have sufficient funds. Try a different payment method. If the issue persists, contact your bank to ensure they are not blocking the transaction.

**I can't see any sitters in search results.**
Try expanding your search radius (increase the distance filter to 50km). Remove any date filters that may be too restrictive. Check that your address is set correctly in Profile > My Address.

**The app is running slowly.**
Close and reopen the app. Ensure you are running the latest version from the App Store or Google Play. Clear the app cache in your device settings if the issue persists.
